Latitude vs Humidity

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 deg latitutde). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of hte year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this date was gathered.
